Who are the KCL Guitar Society?
The KCL Guitar Society was founded by Hani Tawil and Irid Kotoni in 2017. With a univeristy as large as Kings College and a passion for playing the guitar which could not be shared, they started a society where all guitarists and those who are interested in learning can meet and play together. From giving a couple of lessons a week the society has flourished into a active one with an always expanding team of organsizers, teachers and members. We currently host weekly jam sessions for all to attend and provide lessons to new and beginner guitarists.
